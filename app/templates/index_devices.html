Device Data

Overview page of all the devices that have registered with our server. A mobile device is 'registered' with our server when a user 'install/adds' a .pkpass file to their phone. Once added, the mobile device sends a message to our servers for validation checking, and allows the server to correctly identify devices that are associated with a membership, and send updated passes to them. 'Date Registered' is the date when a device has installed their last membership card; indicating to us that they have indeed installed the membership pass at least once to a device. 'Device Library Identifier' is a unique ID associated with the device. The 'Push Token' is used for our server to send any updates to the Apple Push Notifiaction Service (APNs), who will forward the update to the device.
