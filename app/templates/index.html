This is our final projct for 67-442 iOS Mobile Application Development in iOS for Fall 2018 Semester. This iOS appliation seeks to streamline the process for taking attendance through facial recognition. It utilizes both Apple's Core ML framework, and Python's Turicreate library to create & train an image classifer, which can be used to compare images taken from a real-time recording from the iPhone's camera. Ontop of just being able to recognize faces, individuals whose attendance are taken are then committed to a Postgres database, which can be accessed through our Swagger Ruby on Rails API; This allows for future platforms, such as cmuis, to easily obtain attendance records for any class.
